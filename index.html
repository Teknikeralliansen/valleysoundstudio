Discover our world-class recording facilities tucked away among the rolling hills and valleys of the Swedish heartlands.

Owned by Borlänge Municipality and operated by the non-profit Teknikeralliansen, our studio offers a diverse range of technical and creative expertise to elevate your production. From music production to video post-production, voice overs, and game audio - our talented team is ready to bring your vision to life.

The studio features a well equipped control room with multiple monitoring solutions and high-end outboard gear. The performance area consists of a main room, big enough to host a full band, and multiple smaller rooms when separation and different sonic qualities are key.
